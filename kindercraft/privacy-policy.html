KinderCraft — Privacy Policy

Effective: July 2026

KinderCraft is made for children aged 2–8 and their parents. We take children's privacy seriously and designed this app to collect as little information as possible.

1. No personal information

We do NOT ask for or collect any personal information. There is no sign-up, no login, no name, email, photos, location, contacts, or microphone/camera access.

2. On-device only

Your child's progress — stars, badges, and game history — is saved only on your device. It never leaves the phone or tablet and is not sent to us or anyone else. Uninstalling the app removes it.

3. Advertising

The app may show a single banner advertisement (via Google AdMob). Because this is a children's app:

  • Ads are requested as non-personalized only.
  • Requests are tagged as child-directed and for users under the age of consent (COPPA / GDPR-K).
  • Ad content is limited to a family-friendly (G) rating.

Parents can turn ads off any time in Settings → "Show Ads".

4. No data sold or shared

We do not sell, rent, or share any information about you or your child.

5. Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

KinderCraft complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU/UK GDPR rules for children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Third-party services

Google AdMob may process limited, non-identifying technical data to serve non-personalized ads and prevent fraud. See Google's policies at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

7. Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above and the version in the app.

8. Contact

Questions? Email us at support.cubistack@gmail.com.
